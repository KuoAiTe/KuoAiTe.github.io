Scholarships Search Engine

Team members: Ai-Te Kuo, Tian Xia

About us

Matcher is a website intended to provide scholarship and college information for students. Our scholarship search engine is designed for a fast and accurate match for students with a wide range of backgrounds. Our goal is to provide with the best student scholarship search online.

Scenario of use

Jason Mars is a 16 years old high school senior student from Newman High School, Newman, GA. He've competed in Table Tennis League, played at county level for Coweta, and reached the finals of the SEC High School Table Tennis Championships in 2018. He also coaches younger members at his local table tennis club. Now, he plans to go to college next fall in Georgia or Alabama. As he doesn’t have any financial aids yet, he needs to search for financial aids or scholarships/fellowships. He finds the Scholarship website (https://kuoaite.github.io/hw2/college.html) by his high school teacher’s recommendation. He can use this website to search scholarships by different searching criteria and easily find the perfect match for his case.
